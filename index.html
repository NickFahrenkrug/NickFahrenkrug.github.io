I perform new music, opera, song, early music, musical theater, cabaret, and in choral ensembles. I am especially interested in new works, Deep Listening, and experimental performance.

I am cofounder of the Apollo Duo alongside pianist Eleonora Apostolidi.

When I am not singing I enjoy being in nature, tending to my plants, drawing, or learning about new concepts.
