Biography

Baritone Nicholas Fahrenkrug, has recently been awarded an Acknowledgement for Artistic Achievement for his art film “Dichterliebe: Within and Without” from The American Prize. He is a second year Masters student in Vocal Performance at the LSU School of Music, where he studies with Baritone Dennis Jesse. While at LSU he has performed multiple roles with the Turner-Fischer Center for Opera, including Guglielmo (Così fan tutte), Giulio Cesare (Giulio Cesare), Marco (Gianni Schicchi), and Marquis de la Force (Dialogues des Carmélites). Nicholas graduated from Lawrence University Conservatory of Music in 2020, and is originally from Davenport, Iowa.
